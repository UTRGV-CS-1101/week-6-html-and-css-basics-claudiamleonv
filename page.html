Minecraft Doggies

  • Wolves can be tamed with bones and become loyal dogs.
  • Tamed dogs will follow and protect you from hostile mobs.
  • You can dye your dog's collar with any color!
  • Dogs will shake off water after swimming.
  • Right-click your dog to make it sit or stand.
